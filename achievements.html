Our Achievements

Our Achievements Since its inception, TalkCyber360 has remained committed to advancing cybersecurity awareness, education, and professional excellence across Africa. Some of our key achievements include: - Successfully organized two cybersecurity bootcamps, training over 20 aspiring cybersecurity professionals through practical, hands-on sessions. - Delivered security auditing and penetration testing services for two organizations, helping identify and remediate critical security vulnerabilities. - Hosted cybersecurity conferences, webinars, and community events, bringing together industry professionals and technology enthusiasts to discuss emerging cyber threats and innovations. - Built a growing cybersecurity community dedicated to knowledge sharing, mentorship, and professional development. - Launched structured mentorship programs designed to equip learners with practical cybersecurity and ethical hacking skills. - Expanded cybersecurity awareness through educational content, public speaking engagements, and digital media initiatives under the TalkCyber360 brand. As we continue to grow, our mission remains clear: to build a stronger cybersecurity ecosystem by empowering individuals, supporting organizations, and developing the next generation of cybersecurity professionals.
